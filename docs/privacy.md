# Privacy Policy

EveryPolitician is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

## What we collect

We collect minimal data to help us understand how the site is used and to improve it:

- **Usage analytics**: We use privacy-focused analytics to understand which pages are visited and how users navigate the site. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.
- **Server logs**: Our servers automatically log requests, including IP addresses, for security and debugging purposes. These logs are retained for a limited period.

## What we don't do

- We don't sell your data to third parties
- We don't use advertising trackers
- We don't require you to create an account to browse the data

## Public data

The politician data displayed on this site is drawn from public sources and is intended to be freely accessible. If you believe any information is incorrect or should be removed, please [contact us](/contact/).

## Related policies

EveryPolitician is a project of [OpenSanctions](https://opensanctions.org). See also the [OpenSanctions privacy policy](https://opensanctions.org/docs/privacy/).
