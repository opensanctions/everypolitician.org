# Contribute

EveryPolitician is a community project, and we welcome contributions from anyone who wants to help improve coverage of political data.

- [Using PoliLoom](/about/contribute/poliloom/)
- [Editing Wikidata](/about/contribute/wikidata/)
- [Build a data crawler](https://github.com/opensanctions/crawler-planning)
- [Suggest a new data source](https://github.com/opensanctions/crawler-planning/issues/new?template=source_suggestion.yml)
  - Check our [Crawlers project](https://github.com/orgs/opensanctions/projects/2/views/1) as well as [existing proposed sources](https://github.com/opensanctions/crawler-planning/issues?q=is%3Aopen+is%3Aissue+label%3Aproposed) first, to avoid a duplicate suggestion
- [Open an issue](https://github.com/opensanctions/everypolitician.org/issues)
